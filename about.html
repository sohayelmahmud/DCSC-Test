Who We Are

The College Science Club is a student-led organization dedicated to promoting scientific literacy and fostering a love for discovery. Founded in 2010, we have grown into a vibrant community of thinkers, creators, and innovators.

We believe that science is not just a subject but a way of thinking. Our members come from diverse backgrounds but share a common passion for understanding the world around us.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower students to explore the frontiers of science and technology through hands-on learning, collaboration, and public engagement.

What We Do

  • Organize annual science fairs and exhibitions.
  • Conduct workshops on robotics, coding, and astronomy.
  • Host seminars with guest speakers from top universities.
  • Publish a monthly science newsletter.
